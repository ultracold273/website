Biography

I'm now a PhD candidate of Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Qiang Xu. I obtained my B.S. degree from School of Electronic Science and Engineering at Nanjing University at 2012.

 

Research

My research mainly focuses on security topic, especially on hardware security and trusted computing.
